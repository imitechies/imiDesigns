About Us

IMI - Implementmyidea Technologies

IMI is an IT consulting, Solution Implementer, outsourcing and Training provider. Our Team of expert consultants will help you make confident technology decisions and ensure your IT organization get agile, effective and equipped to cut through the buzz of the latest trends to create enduring results. Our services ranges from providing professional services, training, outsourcing and system integration. Founded in 2018, we gradually expanded our business in Asia, Middle East and Europe over the time. Some of our major clients are Oracle Corporation, Wipro, Mobily and more.
